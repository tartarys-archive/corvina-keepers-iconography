# Corvina Keepers – Banners  
## Visual Presence & Context Specification v1.0

**Status:** Canonical (v1)  
**Custody:** Corvina Keepers Community  
**Scope:** Public visual presence, context framing  
**Date:** 2026-01-09 

---

## 1. Purpose of Banners

Banners are **contextual carriers** of the Corvina Keepers identity.

Their role is not promotion, but:
- orientation,
- framing,
- quiet signaling of presence.

A banner invites attention without demanding it.

---

## 2. Core Principles

All Corvina Keepers banners must respect the following principles:

- **Stillness over noise**
- **Symbol over slogan**
- **Space over density**
- **Meaning over marketing**

No banner may function as an advertisement.

---

## 3. Primary Banner Composition

### 3.1 Structure
The primary banner consists of:
- A central **open book** (knowledge / record)
- One **raven on each side**, facing inward
- Horizontal symmetry
- Clear negative space

No text is required for a banner to be valid.

### 3.2 Symbolic Balance
- Ravens act as guardians, not figures of action
- The book remains the visual anchor
- Composition suggests watchfulness, not authority

---

## 4. Dimensions and Formats

### 4.1 Recommended Ratios
- **Wide banner:** 3:1 or 5:2 (e.g. X / web headers)
- **Medium banner:** 2:1 (documents, presentations)
- **Ledger banner:** 16:9 (event records, archive headers)

### 4.2 Resolution
- Minimum: 1500 px width
- Preferred: vector or high-resolution raster

---

## 5. Color System

### 5.1 Approved Backgrounds
- Light parchment gray
- Deep indigo
- Neutral black

### 5.2 Raven Rendering
- Natural dark tones
- No gold or yellow beaks
- No metallic shine

### 5.3 Book Rendering
- Light, readable tones
- Subtle contrast only
- No ornamentation

---

## 6. Text Usage

### 6.1 Default Rule
Banners are **text-free by default**.

### 6.2 Optional Text (Rare)
If text is used, it must:
- be minimal (single line)
- avoid calls to action
- avoid promises or claims

Examples of acceptable text:
- “Corvina Keepers”
- “Ledger Entry”
- “Witnessed”

---

## 7. Prohibited Elements

The following are not allowed on banners:
- Marketing slogans
- Token prices or financial references
- Countdown timers
- Emojis or decorative symbols
- Partner logos

---

## 8. Usage Contexts

### 8.1 Approved Uses
- Social media headers
- GitHub repository headers
- White paper covers
- Ledger milestone entries
- Live ceremony streams

### 8.2 Restricted Uses
- Paid advertising
- Influencer promotions
- Exchange listings without community approval

---

## 9. Relationship to Other Visual Elements

- Banners may include the **Core Logo**, but must not replace it
- Banners must not incorporate the **Ceremonial Seal**
- Banners are contextual, not authoritative symbols

---

## 10. Versioning and Archival

This document defines **Banners v1.0**.

Any new banner variant must:
- be documented,
- timestamped,
- optionally referenced in the Corvina Ledger.

---

## 11. Closing Statement

A Corvina Keepers banner does not announce.  
It **stands**.

Those who understand it, stop.  
Those who do not, pass by.

---

**End of Specification**
