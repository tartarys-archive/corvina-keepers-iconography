# Corvina Keepers – Logos  
## Iconography & Usage Specification v1.0

**Status:** Canonical (v1)  
**Custody:** Corvina Keepers Community  
**Scope:** Visual identity, symbolic consistency, public recognition  
**Date:** 2026-01-09  

---

## 1. Purpose of the Logos

The Corvina Keepers logos serve as **visual anchors** of the project’s identity.

Their purpose is:
- to provide immediate recognizability,
- to express guardianship rather than ownership,
- to visually connect documents, repositories, and ceremonies,
- to ensure consistency across platforms and media.

Logos are **symbols of trust**, not instruments of authority.

---

## 2. Core Logo (Primary Mark)

### 2.1 Structure
The primary Corvina Keepers logo consists of:
- **Two ravens (corvinae)** positioned symmetrically
- An **open book** at the center
- Circular or vertical balance emphasizing stability and guardianship

The ravens:
- face inward toward the book,
- exhibit natural raven head and beak morphology,
- are rendered in a sober, non-aggressive posture.

### 2.2 Symbolic Meaning
- Ravens: memory, vigilance, continuity
- Open book: recorded knowledge, shared heritage
- Symmetry: balance, fairness, accountability

---

## 3. Color System

### 3.1 Primary Palette
- **Deep Indigo / Near-Black** (primary)
- **Light Gray / Parchment White** (background)
- **Natural Black** (raven details)

### 3.2 Prohibited Colors
- Gold or yellow beaks (avoids misidentification with other birds)
- Bright, saturated commercial colors
- Gradients that obscure symbol clarity

---

## 4. Logo Variants

### 4.1 Primary Logo
- Full emblem with ravens and open book
- Used for:
  - white papers
  - ceremonial documentation
  - official repositories

### 4.2 Circular Icon
- Optimized for:
  - social media profile images
  - avatars
  - NFT thumbnails
- Central motif preserved, outer details simplified

### 4.3 Banner Composition
- Wide horizontal format
- Ravens positioned left and right
- Open book centered
- Negative space preserved for legibility

---

## 5. Background Usage

Logos may appear on:
- light gray
- parchment white
- dark indigo
- neutral black

Transparent background versions are encouraged for digital use.

---

## 6. Scaling and Legibility

- Minimum size must preserve:
  - beak shape clarity
  - book spine visibility
- Logos must remain recognizable at small sizes (e.g., 400×400 px)

Distortion, skewing, or cropping of symbolic elements is prohibited.

---

## 7. Usage Guidelines

### 7.1 Allowed Uses
- GitHub repositories
- White papers and standards
- Ledger records
- Social media presence
- Educational and archival materials

### 7.2 Prohibited Uses
- Commercial merchandise without consent
- Token promotion implying financial guarantees
- Association with unrelated projects or ideologies

---

## 8. Custody and Modification

- All official logos are held under **community guardianship**.
- Visual refinements are allowed if:
  - symbolic meaning is preserved,
  - changes are documented,
  - versioning is respected.

Unauthorized modifications invalidate official status.

---

## 9. Relationship to Other Symbols

- The logos are distinct from:
  - the Ceremonial Seal
  - token symbols (Corvina Sigil)
- They may appear together but must not be merged.

---

## 10. Versioning

This document defines **Logos v1.0**.

Future versions may:
- refine proportions,
- add accessibility variants,
- define additional formats.

All changes must be recorded in the **Corvina Ledger**.

---

## 11. Closing Statement

The Corvina Keepers logos do not claim attention.  
They **stand watch**.

They mark continuity, care, and the quiet presence of guardianship.

---

**End of Specification**
